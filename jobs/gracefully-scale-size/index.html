Gracefully Scale Size

Perform well with small or big data.

Increase the speed or the amount of data without spending energy thinking about scaling issues. This means you can start simple and lightweight and still scale up to large volumes.

Context

It happens throughout the whole life of the project.

Initially, you may create solutions that provenly won't scale. Over time, after the project starts to grow, you will want to change the architecture.
