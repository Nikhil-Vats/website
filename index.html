A suite of open tools to help you at every step of the data journey.

Frictionless Data brings simplicity and grace to the data experience by providing a suite of open source tools that help you at every step of your data journey.

Approachable

We keep things as simple as possible. Read the guide and start using and building in no time!

Versatile

An incrementally adoptable ecosystem that scales between a library and a full-featured framework and which works whether you are wrangling an Excel file or working with Terabytes.

Progressive

We work with your existing tooling and data, enhancing and adding to it. – not replacing it!
